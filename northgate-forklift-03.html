Out-of-Service Control

If Forklift 03 is unsafe, damaged or showing a safety-critical fault, mark it out of service before further use.

  • Stop using the forklift immediately.
  • Apply an Out of Service tag where visible.
  • Use Mark Out of Service to update Hold.
  • Notify maintenance or the warehouse supervisor.
  • Do not return equipment to use until release has been recorded.

Workflow Notes

  • Faults raised from Forklift 03 appear in the central operational review queue.
  • Out-of-service status remains visible until formally resolved.
  • Maintenance updates should be added to preserve the equipment audit trail.
  • Equipment must not be returned to service without authorised release.

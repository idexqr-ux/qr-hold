Hold Area Guidance

  • Place items in Hold Area B only where stock is damaged, disputed, unstable, incomplete or awaiting operational decision.
  • Apply the correct hold label before leaving goods in the area.
  • Record the hold reference in Hold before stock leaves the originating bay.
  • Do not return goods to normal workflow until a release decision has been recorded.

Release Criteria

  • Reviewer decision recorded in Hold.
  • Required photographs, notes or paperwork attached where applicable.
  • Stock label updated before movement out of Hold Area B.
  • Destination confirmed: stock, dispatch, supplier return, disposal or further review.

Workflow Notes

  • Items placed in Hold Area B remain visible in the central operational queue until resolved.
  • Open hold items persist across shifts and require formal closure.
  • Updates and comments should be visible to relevant staff.
  • Items must not be released without an authorised review decision.
  • Resolved hold records remain available for audit and review.

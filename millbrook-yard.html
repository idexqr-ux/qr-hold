Governance Controls

  1. Maintain verified utility meter register

  6. Escalate unresolved tenant access disputes

Site Access

  • Contractor routing Large vehicles directed away from restricted estate access points
  • Visitor guidance Temporary parking and access instructions issued during busy operational periods

Communication Controls

Operational Coordination

  1. Issue contractor arrival guidance before attendance

Responsibility Register

Fragmented Responsibilities

  • Shared welfare facilities Historically managed informally by tenant volunteer; now under contracted estate service
  • Visitor parking Shared bays require clearer allocation and tenant communication

Governance Shift

From Informal Memory to Managed Record

  • Historic practice Local knowledge and goodwill previously filled gaps in formal records
  • Current requirement Compliance, liability and tenant change now require visible governance
  • Hold role Preserves inherited knowledge while turning it into usable operational records

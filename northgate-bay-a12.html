Damage & Discrepancy Handling

If goods arrive damaged, short, unstable or different from the manifest, record the issue before the stock enters normal workflow.

  • Photograph damaged or suspect goods before unloading further.
  • Move affected goods to Intake Hold Area B.
  • Apply a Damaged / Pending Review label.
  • Log the issue against the delivery reference using Report Intake Issue.
  • Do not book stock into inventory until the discrepancy is resolved.

Acceptance Criteria

  • Packaging intact and free from visible water damage.
  • Pallet stable and safe to move.
  • Correct quantity received against manifest or delivery note.
  • No signs of tampering or broken seal where applicable.

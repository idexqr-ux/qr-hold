Delivery Intake Station

Operational guidance for receiving inbound deliveries, validating shipment condition, and escalating intake discrepancies.

Status: Active QR Point: GI-01 Version: 2.1 Next Review: 21 July 2026

Delivery Intake Process

  1. Confirm delivery reference against scheduled inbound manifest.
  2. Inspect vehicle seal and trailer condition before unloading.
  3. Unload goods into designated intake zone.
  4. Check pallet count and packaging condition against delivery note.
  5. Record shortages, damage, or discrepancies before acceptance.
  6. Apply intake status label and route goods for inspection/storage.

Acceptance Criteria

  • Packaging intact and free from visible water damage.
  • Pallet stable and safe to move.
  • Correct quantity received against manifest.
  • No signs of tampering or broken seal where applicable.

Damage & Discrepancy Handling

If goods arrive damaged, short, unstable, or different from the manifest, record the issue before the stock enters normal workflow.

  • Photograph damaged or suspect goods before unloading further.
  • Quarantine affected stock in Intake Hold Area B.
  • Apply a Damaged / Pending Review label to the pallet or item.
  • Use the Report an Issue button below to log the discrepancy against the delivery reference.
  • Notify the Goods In Supervisor for acceptance, rejection, or supplier escalation decision.
  • Do not book stock into inventory until the discrepancy is resolved.

QR Control Rules

Operational QR points should not be moved, copied or replaced without an approved Hold record update.

  • New QR requests require approval before deployment.
  • Replacement QRs should preserve the same record link unless formally reassigned.
  • Retired QR codes remain visible in audit history.
  • Damaged, missing or unreliable QR labels should be reported immediately.

Workflow Notes

  • QR requests can be raised from operational pages where staff identify missing point-of-need guidance.
  • Approved QR changes update the registry and remain visible for audit.
  • QR estate management supports continuity when labels are damaged, relocated or retired.
  • QR governance helps ensure physical scan points remain aligned with current operational records.

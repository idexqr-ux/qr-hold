Expiry & Restriction Control

Staff should not operate equipment or perform controlled tasks where required training is expired, restricted or awaiting manager review.

  • Expiry warnings appear before authorisation lapses.
  • Restrictions remain visible until removed by an authorised manager.
  • Refresher training should be scheduled before controlled access is lost.
  • Authorisation changes are retained in the audit trail.

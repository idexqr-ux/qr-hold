Forklift Charging Station

Operational guidance for safe charging, fault reporting, and battery handling at the Northgate Warehouse forklift charging bay.

Status: Active QR Point: EQ-07 Version: 2.4 Next Review: 14 July 2026

Standard Procedure

  1. Park forklift fully within the marked charging bay.
  2. Lower forks to ground level and engage the parking brake.
  3. Turn ignition off before disconnecting the battery.
  4. Inspect the charging cable for visible damage before use.
  5. Connect charger lead securely to the charging port.
  6. Confirm the charging indicator displays green.
  7. Record any faults immediately before leaving the bay.

Safety Requirements

  • Safety gloves must be worn when handling battery connections.
  • Do not use chargers with exposed wiring or damaged plugs.
  • Do not charge forklifts showing signs of battery leakage.
  • The charging bay must remain clear of packaging and combustible materials.

Fault Reporting

If battery damage, leakage, or charger fault is identified, follow the escalation steps below immediately.

1 Stop use immediately and isolate the equipment where safe to do so.
2 Attach an “Out of Service” tag to the affected charger or vehicle.
3 Use the Report an Issue button below to log the maintenance fault.
4 Notify Facilities on extension 4421 for urgent safety concerns.
